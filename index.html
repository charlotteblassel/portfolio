About me

Introduction

I'm a 19-year-old french student in web development, web marketing and web design at HETIC, a school located in France, near Paris.
I've always been interested by the web, in all its aspects, both as a user and now as a maker.

School life
I'm open to every opportunities the school offers me. I took part in the Vivatech fair in Paris in May 2019 as a willing volunteer. I discovered some companies and helped the visitors to find what they were looking for and discover what they could do there.
In November 2019, I started to write articles for the school's communication on the school management's request, with other students from different promotions and branches. I also took part in a 36-hours challenge for Accor group with other schools in the fields of design, marketing and hotel industry.


Languages and trips

I love English and Spanish, either writing or speaking. This allows me to travel a lot. I went to a lot of European countries and cities (London, Nuremberg, Barcelona, Germany, Spain, Poland). I also went to South Africa in August 2019 with my family and discovered Cape town and Johannesburg. I took a lot of photographies and opened up to a new culture.

Hobbies
From 12 to 17 years-old, I was in a theatre group with my school. Since then, I've always loved drama and it really helped me to express myself and be more comfortable in public.
In my free time, I cook a lot.

Development

I learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript at school.
I already made a few websites, either alone or in groups, in a few days or weeks.

Marketing

I've studied corporate communication for 2 years and worked on a few group projects to create different brands and events. This year, I also learn digital marketing.

Design

I learnt to use Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and I'm discovering Adobe XD and Figma.

Humanities

In parallel of the web subjects, I study philosophy, general knowledge and french.
It helped me to improve my reflexion and my written and oral skills.
